Welcome to swing!

If you are tired of the monotony of everyday life, your soul needs a holiday, you want to add to your life a lot of movement, good music, new friends and travel - then you definitely need us! Dancing swing under jazz music from 20s -30s (and not only), regular parties and concerts with live music, beautiful hairstyles, dresses for girls and suits for boys. Feel the charm of fast lindy-hop under the catchy great bands music and the deep intimacy of a harmonica Blues.
